Immediate Needs

All your needs for only $20

Obtaining basic needs is a serious problem for people without homes. Above, we have an efficient way to get what you need for only $20, but the most efficient solution will be Elpida. Get one for free at a shelter near you today, and you will be able to:

  • Input what you need on a case-to-case basis.
  • Locate organizations near you that provide what you need.
  • Find job oppotunities more easily
  • Find places to get treatment for any substance abuse problems or mental health issues (personal informaiton will be kept confidential)
